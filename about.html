About MindSculpt

MindSculpt is a groundbreaking virtual reality game that explores mental health issues by placing players in the role of a psychiatrist navigating the complexities of various disorders. Players experience an emotionally charged journey towards empathy and understanding. The game aims to spread awareness on mental health by immersing players in the experiences of those facing challenges. MindSculpt seeks to inspire meaningful conversations, contributing to the global dialogue on mental health awareness and support.
